Target Audience Description

The audience I am trying to reach are hard working people that want to get straight to the point. I want people that want their answers fast and to the point. People that see a weather statment or the tempature at that time in the day and are satisfied by the site from these basic important things.

Persona One

Efficent User

This user will spend at most 5 mintutes on the site. They will get to the point and gain the data they come for. They want to get straigh to the point to see the highs and lows for the day, wind mph, or other descripters like humitity percentage. They gain the knowlege and then leave the site for the day. They will rinse and repeat most days of the week.

Persona Two

Book Worm

This user will demonstaright an enourmous amount of knowlege and time to gain the data provided on the website. They will look at descripters for the future week. Highs and lows are just the start for this user. They will use some of their time for other things like special weather statments, weather in different surrounding cities, humitity percentages, and percipitation percentages.

Scenarios

Scenario One

A user wants to see the current tempature in thier area and the high and low for the day. The site will display the current tempature and the future tempatures in a outlook display for the rest of the day goint until midnight.

Scenario Two

A user wants to see if it is going to rain on the weekend of their outdoor concert. The website displays there is a 20% change of rain throughout the day. This isnt great news but at least its less than 100%.

Scenario Three

A user is tired of being so hot while doing their errands and being outside part of the day. They go onto the website and look at the future forcasts for the next week. They see a cole front will come in so they plan their errands for that day of the week.

Scenario Four

A user is planning a cookout for the football game later that day. They see some clouds in the sky and check if there is a rain percentage that day for when he is doing the cookout. There is a 10% rian chance tahnkfully.

Scenario Five

A user is experiencing an extreme strom and wants to see if they should prepare for anything like hail or a tornado. They look at the website and there is an extreme thrunderstorm warning for their county and reports of some possible hail damage accross their city. They park their cars in the garage when they see this.
