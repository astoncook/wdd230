Austin's History

In 1839 the city of Waterloo was chosen to be the capital of the Republic of Texas. This city was quickly built and renamed after Stephen F. Austin. In 1888 the capital was built and was the 7th largest building in the world at the time. Austin had some rough fallouts when the Great Depression hit, but the city just had a little bump in the road.

By the 1950s Austin was becoming one of the hot spots for technology and new infustries. This trend went and continued throughout the 1900s and starting in the first decade of the 2000s Austin quickly became a hotspot for technology and new oppertunities. Recently we got the news that Tesla was moving their headquarters to Austin and the riches man in the world, Elon Musk would be living here full time while working on the comany.
