Design Principles Week 2

Visual Hierarchy

Apple

Apple.com

Apple's webiste shares wonderful design tactics that hold well to this day. We can see their new iPhone (the iPhone 13) this the new focal point of their website. This puts a lot of attention on this object for the views to have a high chance of clicking on this element.

Rule of Thirds

Royal Caribbean

Royal Caribbean.com

Royal Caribbean's website shows us multiple examples of the rule of thirds. In this examples it shows the ship on the right half of the screen and makes the ocean a great focus background. The left side is used for the hyperlink button to click now and book a cruise with them.

White Space and Clean Design

Google

Google.com

Google's website shows white space and a very simple design. It is centered around the search bar and there isnt much complexity from there. Google shows us how white space can be perfected and used in the moddern world today.
